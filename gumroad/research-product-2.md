**Price:** $34 CAD
**What it is:** A comprehensive Google Sheets system for freelancers containing: client CRM tracker, project pipeline, invoice generator, income/expense tracker, tax prep worksheet, proposal template, and rate calculator.

## 4. THE #1 PICK

### Winner: Candidate A - "The Freelancer's Business-in-a-Box" (Google Sheets Bundle)

**Full Justification:**

**Why this wins over the others:**

1. **Largest addressable market.** There are 76.4 million freelancers in the US alone (2024). Every single one needs business admin tools. This dwarfs the SaaS founder market (Candidate C) and is more defensible than the "make money" space (Candidate B).

2. **Clear gap in the market.** Current Gumroad offerings for freelancer spreadsheets are either: (a) single-purpose basic trackers at $5-$10, or (b) Notion templates that require learning Notion. A COMPREHENSIVE Google Sheets system at $34 occupies an underserved middle ground.

3. **Google Sheets is the right format.** Unlike Notion templates (which require a Notion account and learning curve), Google Sheets is universally accessible, works offline, and is already the tool most freelancers use for basic tracking. Zero friction.

4. **AI can build it entirely overnight.** Every component (formulas, conditional formatting, data validation, dashboard charts, instruction text) can be generated by an AI agent. No design tools, no video recording, no manual work needed.

5. **Strong organic discovery potential.** Search terms like "freelancer client tracker template," "freelance income tracker spreadsheet," "freelance invoice template google sheets" have consistent search volume and low competition on Gumroad specifically.

6. **Complements existing portfolio perfectly.** The prompt toolkit targets developers, the n8n workflow targets automation users, the coloring book guide targets side hustlers. A freelancer business toolkit targets an entirely new audience segment.

7. **No maintenance required.** Spreadsheet formulas don't break, don't need updates, don't expire. Once built, it sells forever.

8. **Natural upsell path.** Freelancers who buy this are exactly the audience who might also buy the Developer Prompt Toolkit (if they're dev freelancers) or the Coloring Book guide (if they're creative freelancers).

## 5. PRODUCT SPEC

### Product Name
**"The Freelancer's Business-in-a-Box: Complete Google Sheets System"**

### Subtitle/Tagline
"Stop losing clients, money, and time. One spreadsheet system to run your entire freelance business."

### Price
- **Regular:** $34 CAD (~$25 USD)
- **Launch discount:** $24 CAD for first 50 buyers
- **Consider:** Tiered pricing - $34 base / $49 with video walkthrough

### Target Audience
- Freelance developers, designers, writers, marketers, consultants
- Solopreneurs running service-based businesses
- New freelancers who are disorganized and losing money
- Freelancers currently using scattered notes/docs with no system

### What's Included (Deliverables)

**Main Product: Google Sheets Workbook (7 tabs)**

1. **Dashboard** - At-a-glance view of active clients, monthly revenue, pipeline value, overdue invoices, upcoming deadlines
2. **Client CRM** - Client name, contact info, status (lead/active/past), project history, notes, follow-up dates, lifetime value calculation
3. **Project Pipeline** - Project name, client, status (prospect/proposal/active/review/complete), deadline, quoted price, actual hours, hourly rate analysis
4. **Invoice Tracker** - Invoice #, client, amount, date sent, date due, date paid, status (pending/paid/overdue), automatic overdue highlighting
5. **Income & Expenses** - Monthly income log, categorized expenses (software, equipment, marketing, etc.), profit calculation, tax estimate (quarterly)
6. **Rate Calculator** - Annual income goal, working hours, vacation days, overhead costs = required hourly rate. Includes project-based pricing calculator.
7. **Proposal Template** - Fill-in-the-blank proposal structure with scope, timeline, pricing, terms sections

**Bonus Files:**

8. **Quick Start Guide (PDF, 5 pages)** - How to make a copy, how to customize, walkthrough of each tab
9. **Freelancer Tax Prep Checklist (PDF, 2 pages)** - Quarterly/annual tax prep steps, what to track, deduction categories
10. **Client Onboarding Checklist (PDF, 1 page)** - Steps to onboard a new client properly

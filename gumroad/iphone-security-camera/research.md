## 2. Common Problems Repurposing Old iPhones

1. **Wi-Fi Connectivity** — Drops, weak signal, interference. Fix: Wi-Fi extender, mesh network, channel optimization
2. **Overheating** — Continuous use causes thermal throttling. Fix: ventilation, avoid sunlight, cooling stand
3. **App Crashes** — iOS compatibility, storage full. Fix: lightweight apps, clear storage, check compatibility
4. **Storage Limitations** — Continuous recording fills storage fast. Fix: motion-triggered recording, cloud storage, auto-delete
5. **Battery Degradation** — Old batteries can't hold charge. Fix: keep plugged in, replace battery if needed
6. **iOS Compatibility** — New apps may not support old iOS. Fix: check requirements, use compatible apps
7. **Placement Challenges** — Mounting, angles, lighting. Fix: phone mounts, adhesive strips, tripods
8. **Screen Burn-in** — Always-on display damage. Fix: turn off display, use OLED-safe settings
9. **Privacy/Legal** — Recording laws vary by jurisdiction. Fix: research local laws, post notices

## 3. Battery & Charging Best Practices

1. **Optimal Charge Range**: Keep between 20-80% when possible
2. **Enable Optimized Charging**: Settings > Battery > Optimized Charging
3. **Heat Management**: Well-ventilated area, avoid sunlight, cooling accessories
4. **Quality Cables**: MFi-certified or Apple cables with 20W+ adapters
5. **Power Cycling**: Restart weekly to clear memory and reset processes
6. **Smart Chargers**: Use chargers that stop at 80% (Belkin, Anker)
7. **Low Power Mode**: Use sparingly — may interfere with camera functionality
8. **Battery Health Check**: Settings > Battery > Battery Health, replace if below 80%
9. **Surge Protection**: Use power strip with surge protection
10. **Wi-Fi over Cellular**: Reduces battery drain from signal scanning

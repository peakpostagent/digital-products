# Chapter 3: Camera Setup (Step-by-Step)

This chapter provides complete setup instructions for our top three recommended apps. Follow the guide for whichever app you chose in Chapter 1.

## Alfred Camera Setup

Alfred Camera is the fastest to set up. You'll need two devices: the old iPhone (camera) and your primary phone (viewer).

### On your primary phone (viewer):

1. Download **Alfred Camera** from the App Store or Google Play
2. Open the app and sign in with your Google account
3. The app will ask which role this device plays — select **Viewer**
4. You'll see a dashboard with a QR code option

### On your old iPhone (camera):

1. Download **Alfred Camera** from the App Store
2. Open the app and sign in with the **same Google account**
3. Select **Camera** as the device role
4. Grant permissions when prompted:
   - Camera access: **Allow**
   - Microphone access: **Allow**
   - Notifications: **Allow**
5. The camera feed should appear immediately
6. On your viewer phone, you should now see the camera listed on the dashboard

### Configure Alfred Camera settings:

On the camera phone, tap the settings gear icon:

- **Motion Detection**: Turn **on**
- **Motion Sensitivity**: Start with **Medium** (adjust later based on false alerts)
- **Camera Selection**: Choose **Back camera** for better quality (front camera for self-facing)
- **Resolution**: Set to **480p** for battery savings or **720p** for better quality
- **Audio**: Turn **on** if you want sound recording
- **Low Light Filter**: Turn **on** if the area has dim lighting

### Test your setup:

1. Walk in front of the camera
2. Check your viewer phone — you should receive a motion alert within 10-30 seconds
3. Open the Alfred app on your viewer to see the live feed
4. Test two-way audio by tapping the microphone icon on the viewer

## Presence Setup

Presence takes slightly longer to configure but offers more advanced features.

### On your old iPhone (camera):

1. Download **Presence** from the App Store
2. Create an account (email and password)
3. Open the app and select **Use as Camera**
4. Grant permissions:
   - Camera: **Allow**
   - Microphone: **Allow**
   - Notifications: **Allow**
   - Location: **Allow** (used for home/away automation)
5. The camera feed will start immediately

### On your primary phone (viewer):

1. Download **Presence** from the App Store or Google Play
2. Sign in with the **same account**
3. Select **Use as Viewer**
4. Your camera should appear in the device list

### Configure Presence settings:

On the camera phone:

- **Motion Detection**: Turn **on**
- **Smart Detection**: Turn **on** (enables AI person/vehicle detection)
- **Sensitivity**: Start with **Medium**
- **Night Mode**: Turn **on** if the area has low lighting
- **Video Quality**: Select **Standard** for battery savings, **High** for better quality
- **Cloud Recording**: Enable if you want automatic clip storage
- **Two-Way Audio**: Turn **on**

### Set up smart alerts:

1. On the viewer phone, go to **Settings > Alerts**
2. Enable **Person Detection** to only get alerts when a person is detected
3. Set **Alert Schedule** if you only want alerts during certain hours
4. Configure **Alert Sound** to your preference

## AtHome Camera Setup

AtHome Camera uses a simple pairing process with a connection ID.

### On your old iPhone (camera):

1. Download **AtHome Camera** from the App Store
2. Open the app — it will automatically start in camera mode
3. Grant permissions:
   - Camera: **Allow**
   - Microphone: **Allow** (optional — no two-way audio, but enables sound detection)
4. Note the **Connection ID (CID)** displayed on screen — you'll need this

### On your primary phone (viewer):

1. Download **AtHome Video Streamer** (the viewer companion app) from the App Store or Google Play
2. Open the app and tap **Add Camera**
3. Enter the **CID** from your camera phone
4. Alternatively, scan the QR code shown on the camera phone
5. The camera should connect and show the live feed

### Configure AtHome settings:

On the camera phone:

- **Motion Detection**: Turn **on**
- **Sensitivity**: Start with **Medium**
- **Recording Mode**: Select **Motion-triggered** (saves storage)
- **Video Quality**: Select **Standard** (saves battery and storage)
- **Storage Location**: Select **Local** (saves to the iPhone's storage)

### Storage management:

Since AtHome uses local storage only:
- Check available storage: **Settings > General > iPhone Storage**
- Enable **Auto-delete old recordings** in the app settings
- Set retention period to **7 days** (or whatever fits your storage)
- Manually review and delete footage during your weekly maintenance

## Post-setup checklist (all apps)

After setting up your chosen app, verify everything works:

- [ ] Live feed visible on viewer phone
- [ ] Motion detection triggering alerts
- [ ] Alerts arriving on viewer phone as push notifications
- [ ] Video quality acceptable
- [ ] Audio working (if applicable)
- [ ] Camera angle captures the desired area
- [ ] App stays active when phone is locked / screen is off

## Troubleshooting first-time setup

**Camera feed not showing on viewer:**
- Make sure both devices are signed into the same account
- Check that both devices have internet access
- Close and reopen the app on both devices
- Check that camera permissions are granted

**Not receiving motion alerts:**
- Verify motion detection is turned on in the camera app
- Check that notifications are enabled for the app (Settings > Notifications)
- Make sure Do Not Disturb allows the camera app notifications

**Poor video quality:**
- Clean the camera lens on the old iPhone
- Adjust video resolution in app settings
- Make sure there's adequate lighting
- Check Wi-Fi signal strength at the camera location

**App crashes or freezes:**
- Close all other apps on the camera phone
- Restart the camera phone
- Reinstall the camera app
- Check that iOS is updated

QUICK SETUP (3 minutes)
------------------------------------------------------

1. Open 30-Day-Habit-Tracker.xlsx in Google Sheets:
   - Go to drive.google.com
   - Click "New" > "File upload"
   - Upload the .xlsx file
   - Double-click to open it
   - It will open in Google Sheets automatically

2. Go to the "Setup" tab:
   - Enter your name and start date
   - Define your habits (start with 3-4, not all 8!)
   - Fill in the category, daily goal, and why it matters

3. Go to the "30-Day Tracker" tab:
   - Each day, enter 1 for completed or 0 for missed
   - Leave future days blank
   - Your streaks and completion rates update automatically

4. Check the "Dashboard" tab anytime to see your progress

5. At the end of each week, use "Weekly Review" to reflect

------------------------------------------------------
TIPS FOR SUCCESS
------------------------------------------------------

- Start with EASY habits (see the Habit Ideas Bank tab)
- Track at the same time each day (set a phone reminder)
- Read the Quick-Start Guide for the science behind habits
- If you miss a day, just enter 0 and keep going
- Share the spreadsheet with a friend for accountability

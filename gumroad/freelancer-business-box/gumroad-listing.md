## Full Description

### Stop Juggling 5 Different Tools to Run Your Freelance Business

You became a freelancer to do great work, not to spend your evenings updating spreadsheets, chasing invoices, and guessing what to charge.

**The Freelancer's Business-in-a-Box** is a single, professionally designed Google Sheets workbook that replaces your scattered notes, half-filled spreadsheets, and that pricing calculator you bookmarked but never used.

Open it. Fill in your info. Run your business.

---

### What You Get

**7-Tab Google Sheets Workbook (.xlsx)**

1. **Dashboard** -- See your entire business at a glance. Revenue, outstanding invoices, active projects, monthly trends. Every number updates automatically as you enter data in the other tabs.

2. **Client CRM** -- Track every client relationship in one place. Contact info, status, lead source, lifetime value (auto-calculated), and follow-up reminders with color-coded alerts for overdue contacts.

3. **Project Pipeline** -- Manage projects from lead to completion. Dropdown menus for status and priority. Auto-calculated project values and amounts due. Color-coded by status so you can see your pipeline health instantly.

4. **Invoice Tracker** -- Never lose track of an invoice again. Track sent, paid, and overdue invoices with auto-calculated "days to pay" and running totals. Spot late-paying clients before they become a pattern.

5. **Income & Expenses** -- Complete financial tracking with categorized income and expenses. Running profit/loss calculation, tax-deductible expense totals, and monthly summaries. Your accountant will thank you at tax time.

6. **Rate Calculator** -- Stop guessing what to charge. Enter your desired income, expenses, and tax rate. Get your minimum hourly rate, suggested rate with profit margin, day rate, weekly rate, and monthly retainer rate. Includes a comparison table at different utilization levels and a "what if" section for quick scenario planning.

7. **Proposal Template** -- A print-ready proposal layout with sections for scope of work, timeline, pricing, payment terms, and terms and conditions. Fill in the details, export as PDF, and send. Looks professional without any design skills.

**Bonus Materials:**

- Quick Start Guide -- Step-by-step setup instructions with formula explanations
- Tax Prep Checklist -- Know exactly what to track and when for stress-free tax season
- Client Onboarding Checklist -- Kick off every project like a pro with this repeatable process

### Built for Real Freelancers

Every formula, dropdown, and layout choice was made with actual freelance workflows in mind:

- **Dropdown menus** prevent typos and keep your data clean
- **Conditional formatting** highlights what needs your attention (overdue invoices, past-due follow-ups, high-priority projects)
- **Cross-tab formulas** mean you enter data once and it flows everywhere -- the Dashboard pulls from all other tabs automatically
- **Professional formatting** with a clean blue/navy color scheme that looks good in client meetings
- **Sample data included** so you can see how everything works before adding your own info

A: Just a Google account (for Google Sheets) or Microsoft Excel. No add-ons, plugins, or extra software needed.

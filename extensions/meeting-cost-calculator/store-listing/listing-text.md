# Chrome Web Store Listing — Meeting Cost Calculator

## Extension Name
Meeting Cost Calculator

## Short Description (132 chars max)
See the real cost of every meeting on Google Calendar. Shows live cost based on attendees, duration, and hourly rates.

## Detailed Description
Ever wonder how much that meeting actually costs? Meeting Cost Calculator shows you the real dollar cost of every event on your Google Calendar.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Set your hourly rate (or annual salary) in the extension popup
2. Set a default rate for other meeting attendees
3. Click on any calendar event to see its total cost

FEATURES:
- Instant cost calculation for any Google Calendar event
- Supports hourly rate or annual salary input (auto-converts)
- 7 currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, INR
- Live running cost for meetings that are currently in progress
- Shows cost per person and cost per minute breakdown
- Clean, floating badge that doesn't block calendar UI
- Shadow DOM overlay won't break Google Calendar styling
- All data stored locally on your device

WHO IT'S FOR:
- Managers who want to reduce unnecessary meetings
- Teams practicing meeting hygiene
- Freelancers tracking billable time
- Anyone curious about the true cost of meetings

PRIVACY FIRST:
- All calculations happen locally in your browser
- No data is ever sent to external servers
- No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking
- No analytics or cookies

FREE TO USE:
Meeting Cost Calculator is completely free with no hidden costs.

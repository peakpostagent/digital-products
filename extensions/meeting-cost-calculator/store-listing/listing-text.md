# Chrome Web Store Listing — Meeting Cost Calculator v1.1.0

## Extension Name
Meeting Cost Calculator

## Short Description (132 chars max)
See the real cost of every meeting, track weekly spend, rate meeting value, and catch expensive recurring calendar events.

## Detailed Description

Ever wonder how much that meeting actually costs? Meeting Cost Calculator shows you the real dollar cost of every event on your Google Calendar — and now tracks trends, rates meeting value, and projects the annual cost of recurring meetings.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Set your hourly rate (or annual salary) in the extension popup
2. Set a default rate for other meeting attendees
3. Click any calendar event to see its cost, annual projection, and rate its value

WHAT'S NEW IN v1.1.0:

Recurring Meeting Annual Cost — The extension now detects recurring events (weekly standups, monthly all-hands) and projects their annual cost. That weekly 30-minute standup with 10 people could be costing $78,000 a year.

Weekly Cost Dashboard — See your total meeting spend this week, average cost per meeting, most expensive meeting, and week-over-week comparison. A 4-week bar chart shows your trend.

Meeting Value Ratings — Rate meetings as "Valuable," "Somewhat," or "Could've Been an Email." See your Meeting Efficiency Score at a glance to identify which meetings to push back on.

CORE FEATURES:
- Instant cost calculation for any Google Calendar event
- Live running cost for meetings in progress
- Supports hourly rate or annual salary (auto-converts)
- 7 currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, INR
- Cost per person and cost per minute breakdown
- Clean floating badge that doesn't block calendar UI
- Shadow DOM overlay — won't break Google Calendar styling
- All data stored locally on your device

WHO IT'S FOR:
- Managers who want to quantify meeting overhead
- Teams practicing meeting hygiene
- Freelancers tracking billable vs meeting time
- Anyone tired of calendars full of expensive meetings

PRIVACY FIRST:
- All calculations happen locally in your browser
- No data is ever sent to external servers
- No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking
- Data older than 8 weeks is automatically cleaned up

FREE TO USE:
Meeting Cost Calculator is completely free with no hidden costs.

## Single Purpose Description
Calculates and displays the monetary cost of Google Calendar meetings based on attendee count, duration, and configurable hourly rates. Tracks weekly spend, detects recurring meeting annual cost, and records user ratings of meeting value.

## Permission Justifications

**storage** — Stores user settings (hourly rates, currency), meeting cost history for weekly dashboard, and user-provided meeting value ratings locally.

**alarms** — Periodically cleans up meeting cost data older than 8 weeks from local storage to prevent storage bloat.

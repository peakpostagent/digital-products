# Chrome Web Store Listing — Job Match Score

## Extension Name
Job Match Score

## Short Description (132 chars max)
See how well your resume matches any LinkedIn job listing. Get instant match scores with keyword analysis.

## Detailed Description (for CWS listing)
Stop guessing whether you're a good fit. Job Match Score instantly analyzes LinkedIn job listings and shows you exactly how well your resume matches — right on the page.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Paste your resume into the extension popup
2. Browse LinkedIn job listings as usual
3. See your match score badge appear on every job you view

FEATURES:
- Instant match score (0-100%) on every LinkedIn job listing
- Smart keyword extraction from your resume and job descriptions
- Color-coded badge: green (strong match), yellow (partial), red (weak)
- Click the badge to see which keywords matched and which are missing
- Resume keywords saved locally on your device (never sent to any server)
- Works on linkedin.com/jobs pages automatically
- Synonym matching: "Node.js" matches "NodeJS", "Python" matches "py"
- Multi-word skill detection: "machine learning", "data science", etc.
- False-positive prevention: "Java" won't match "JavaScript"

WHO IT'S FOR:
- Job seekers who want to quickly filter relevant listings
- Career changers who want to identify skill gaps
- Anyone applying to multiple positions on LinkedIn

PRIVACY FIRST:
- All processing happens locally in your browser
- Your resume data never leaves your device
- No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking
- No data is collected or transmitted

FREE TO USE:
Job Match Score is completely free with no hidden costs.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Job Match Score is a Chrome extension that helps job seekers see how well their resume matches LinkedIn job listings.

The short version: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

What Data We Access

Job Match Score accesses the following data solely within your browser:

  • Your resume text: The text you paste into the extension popup is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). It never leaves your browser.
  • LinkedIn job descriptions: When you visit a LinkedIn job listing page, the extension reads the visible job description text on the page to compare it against your saved resume keywords. This text is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted.

What Data We Collect

None. Job Match Score does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No cookies
  • No user accounts or sign-ups
  • No network requests to external servers
  • No browsing history collection
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is ever transmitted

Where Data Is Stored

Your resume text and extracted keywords are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage via chrome.storage.local. This data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is only accessible to the Job Match Score extension
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the extension's data or uninstalling it
  • Is not synced across devices (not using chrome.storage.sync)

Third-Party Services

Job Match Score does not use any third-party services, APIs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Used to save your resume text locally so you don't have to re-enter it each time.
  • activeTab: Used to read the job description text from the currently active LinkedIn job listing page.
  • Host permission (linkedin.com/jobs/*): Allows the content script to run on LinkedIn job pages to analyze descriptions and show the match score badge.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

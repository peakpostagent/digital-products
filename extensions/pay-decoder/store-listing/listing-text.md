# Pay Decoder — Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
Pay Decoder

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Instantly decode salary figures on job boards. See hourly, monthly, biweekly, and annual equivalents — all calculated locally.

## Detailed Description

Job listings show salaries in all kinds of formats — annual, hourly, monthly, ranges. Pay Decoder instantly converts them so you can compare apples to apples.

### HOW IT WORKS

1. Browse job listings on LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor
2. Pay Decoder automatically detects salary figures on the page
3. A small inline badge appears next to each salary showing the converted rates
4. Annual salaries show the hourly, monthly, and biweekly equivalents
5. Hourly rates show the annual and monthly equivalents

### FEATURES

- Automatic detection of salary figures in job listings
- Supports annual, hourly, monthly, weekly, biweekly, and daily rates
- Handles salary ranges (e.g., $80k - $120k)
- Recognizes multiple currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, INR
- Customizable work schedule (hours per week, weeks per year)
- Toggle conversions on/off from the popup
- Subtle, non-intrusive inline display that won't break page layouts
- Works on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor

### WHO IT'S FOR

- Job seekers comparing offers with different pay structures
- Freelancers evaluating contract vs. full-time rates
- Recruiters who need quick salary conversions
- Anyone who wants to understand what a salary really means per hour, per month, or per year

### PRIVACY FIRST

Pay Decoder does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your data. All salary calculations happen entirely in your browser. There are no analytics, no tracking, no network requests to external servers, and no accounts required. Your browsing activity and salary data never leave your device.

Privacy Policy — Color Contrast Checker

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Summary

Color Contrast Checker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

Color Contrast Checker collects no personal information. The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your preference toggles (overlay visibility, element highlighting).
  • The result of your most recent contrast check (two color values and a ratio). No URLs, page content, or browsing activity are recorded.

Data sharing

Color Contrast Checker does not transmit any data to external servers, analytics services, or third parties. There are no network requests made by this extension.

Data retention

Only the most recent contrast check result is stored. You can clear all stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing its storage through Chrome's extension settings.

Permissions

Color Contrast Checker requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — To inspect the colors of elements on the page you are currently viewing when you activate the checker.
  • storage — To save your settings and last check result locally.

Third-party services

Color Contrast Checker uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or tracking tools.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be noted here with a revised "last updated" date.
